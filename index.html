Who I am.

My name is Andy Bridger and I am currently a student studying kinesiology at Arizona State University. The fitness industry has set up the illusion that one must give up all that they love in order to attain a "Hollywood level" physique. My philosophy however, is just the opposite. I know for a fact that it is possible to live a stress free life, all while trying to attain the body of your dreams. My whole life I have thought: If you have to give up all that you enjoy in order to have a great body, then how do you expect to enjoy your life once you do have it?

Excercise

Would you believe me if I told you I don't excercise every day? I work out 3 times a week now. And as you can see, I can still do everything (and more) that people can do going 5-6 days a week. So if you're someone who thinks they don't have time, think again! You can get faster, stronger, and better without going to the gym every day.

Diet

I have been utilizing intermittent fasting for almost 2 years now and it has been hands down the most effective nutrition hack I have ever come across. Dieting with IF is fun and it's always freaking delicious! I enjoy what I eat without a boring, strenuous diet.

Self Love

I have always a been firm believer that change comes from within. I teach all my clients to love their body in all stages of progression. Change is difficult, slow, and sometimes painful. But with an open mind and optimistic attitude, anything is possible!
